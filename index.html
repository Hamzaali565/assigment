The Story In The Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour now. On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature, New Scientist, and all the scientific journals her work had appeared in, was awell thumbed copy of On The Road. It had been Molly's favorite Book since college, and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had apent the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote proclaiming that Work is refuge of people who have nothing better to do. Although many considered her pioneering work, unraveling the secrets of the llama DNA, to be an outstanding achievement, Molly did think she had something better to do.
